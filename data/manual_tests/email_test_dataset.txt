Benign Email Samples

1. Benign (level of toughness: very easy – completely harmless)
Subject: Meeting Reminder – Project Sync
Body:
Hi team,
This is a reminder that our weekly project sync is scheduled tomorrow at 10 AM in Conference Room B.
Please be on time.
Regards,
Sudarshan

2. Benign (level of toughness: easy)
Subject: Receipt for Your Recent Purchase
Body:
Hello,
Attached is the receipt for your order at TechMart.
Let us know if you need any assistance.
Thank you,
TechMart Support

3. Benign (level of toughness: medium – contains a URL but legitimate)
Subject: Your University Portal Login Link
Body:

You can access your semester grade report at the official portal:
https://my.university.edu/login
Best regards,
Academic Services

Phishing Email Samples

1. Phishing (level of toughness: easy – obvious phishing)
Subject: Your Account Will Be Deleted in 24 Hours
Body:
Dear user,
Your account is scheduled for immediate deletion.
Prevent loss of data by logging in now:
http://secure-verify-account-now.xyz
Support Team

2. Phishing (level of toughness: medium)
Subject: Unusual Login Attempt – Verify Immediately
Body:
Hello,
We detected a login attempt from a new device. Please confirm your identity to avoid suspension:
https://login-security-alert-check.com/verify
Thank you,
Security Team

3. Phishing (level of toughness: strong & subtle)
Subject: Payment Confirmation Needed
Body:
Hi,
We were unable to process your latest payment. To avoid interruption of services, kindly review your billing details at:
https://billing-update-secureportal.com/session
Best regards,
Billing Support

4. Phishing (level of toughness: very strong – disguised brand)
Subject: Important: Update Your Microsoft 365 Credentials
Body:
Dear Sudarshan,
Your Microsoft 365 access has been restricted due to an expired security token.
Restore access here:
https://login-m365-authverify.com/reset
Regards,
Microsoft Account Team

5. Phishing (level of toughness: subtle & short)
Subject: Invoice #88322
Body:
Your invoice is attached. Please review to avoid penalty charges:
https://invoice-check-fast.com
Accounts

6. Phishing (level of toughness: medium)
Subject: Scholarship Application Incomplete
Body:
Hello Sudarshan,
Your scholarship form is incomplete. Submit your details now to avoid rejection:
http://university-scholarship-verify.com
Admin Office

7. Phishing (level of toughness: very strong – realistic)
Subject: UM-Dearborn: Action Required on Your Student Portal

Please authenticate using the link below:
https://um-dearborn-student-access.com/login

Provide details here to reschedule delivery:
